Super Speciality

Bariatric Surgery

Our experienced surgeons offer a range of weight loss procedures to help you achieve your weight management goals.

Cardiology

Our cardiologists specialize in diagnosing and treating heart conditions, such as heart attacks, heart failure, arrhythmias, and high blood pressure.

Gastroenterology

Our gastroenterologists diagnose and treat conditions affecting the digestive system, including ulcers, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), colon cancer, and liver diseases. We offer advanced procedures like colonoscopy and endoscopy.

Interventional Radiology

Our interventional radiologists use minimally invasive techniques to diagnose and treat a variety of medical conditions, including vascular diseases, tumors, and musculoskeletal disorders.

Neonatology

Our neonatologists provide specialized care for newborns, including premature babies and those with health complications. We have state-of-the-art neonatal intensive care units (NICUs) equipped with the latest technology.

Nephrology

Our nephrologists diagnose and treat kidney diseases, including kidney stones, kidney failure, and chronic kidney disease. We offer dialysis services and kidney transplant evaluations.

Neurology

Our neurologists specialize in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including stroke, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, and epilepsy. We offer advanced diagnostic tests and treatment options.

Neuro Surgery

Our neurosurgeons perform surgical procedures to treat conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system, such as brain tumors, spinal cord injuries, and herniated discs.

Oncology

Our oncologists provide comprehensive cancer care, including diagnosis, treatment, and support services. We offer a variety of treatment options, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgery.

Paediatric Surgery

Our pediatric surgeons specialize in surgical procedures for children, addressing a wide range of conditions, such as congenital defects, injuries, and childhood cancers.

Reproductive Medicine

Our reproductive medicine specialists offer fertility treatments, including in vitro fertilization (IVF), intrauterine insemination (IUI), and preimplantation genetic testing (PGT). We also perform surgical procedures related to reproductive health.

Rheumatology

Our rheumatologists diagnose and treat autoimmune and rheumatic diseases affecting the joints, muscles, and connective tissues, such as arthritis, lupus, and fibromyalgia.

Spine Surgery

Our spine surgeons specialize in treating conditions affecting the spine, including back pain, herniated discs, and spinal deformities. We offer a variety of surgical procedures, including spinal fusion and laminectomy.

Urology

Our urologists diagnose and treat diseases and conditions of the urinary tract and reproductive organs, including kidney stones, urinary tract infections, prostate cancer, and erectile dysfunction.
